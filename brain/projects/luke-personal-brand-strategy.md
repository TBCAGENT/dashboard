## Content Strategy (Research-Based)

### What Actually Goes Viral in Real Estate:
1. Property analysis breakdowns (15-30 seconds)
2. "Red flags that made me walk away" content
3. Behind-the-scenes deal hunting
4. "Here's how I actually do it" vs theory
5. Market insights from real activity

### Perfect Workflow for Luke:
- Film existing 15hr/week underwriting sessions
- Screen record + face cam setup
- Overseas editor creates 20+ clips per week
- Post 3x daily across platforms
- Zero additional time investment needed

### Alex Hormozi Formula Applied:
- Document real work vs teaching theory
- "Here's how I did it" not "Here's how you should do it"
- Give away secrets, sell implementation
- Volume approach: 80+ pieces per week
- Build credibility through actual results

## Key Insights from Research

- TikTok algorithm favors geographic content (perfect for Detroit focus)
- Authenticity beats production value
- Behind-the-scenes content outperforms polished content
- "How I did it" outperforms "How to do it" by massive margins
- Volume is crucial - need 3+ posts daily for algorithm success
- Real results create unquestionable authority
